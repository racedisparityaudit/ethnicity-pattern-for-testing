Buy a fishing rod licence

You can buy a 1-day, 8-day or 12-month licence online.

You’ll need:

  • a debit or credit card
  • your Blue Badge or National Insurance number, if you have a disability and you’re applying for a 12-month licence
  • the other person’s details (such as date of birth), if you’re buying for someone else
